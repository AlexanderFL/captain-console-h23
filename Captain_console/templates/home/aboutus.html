About Us

At Captain Console, we're crazy about retro games and have an undying love for what we consider to have been the golden years of gaming.

To us, these games were actually fun to play, which is a far cry from the dull and repetitive nature of modern games that are meant to capture a wider casual audience, purely for the profit. Whether it's the obsession with graphics or the countless DLCs, we are simply sick and tired of modern games.

We believe newer generations, who are not fortunate enough to have experienced the golden years of gaming, will finally have the chance to do so thanks to this site.
